PIU Score Extractor — anchored PoC

Two-pass, all in your browser (Tesseract.js / WASM, no server/API/cost). Pass 1 finds the labels (which OCR reads reliably). Pass 2 crops the digit next to each label and reads just that strip, digits-only. Then the score is reconstructed from the judgements and cross-checked. Watch the per-field crops to see localization working.
